  ### Insurance Box Content (EN) — Implemented 4/3/2026

  **Auto**
  - Description: Auto insurance is designed to protect you, your vehicle, and others on the road.
  - Coverage Options: Bodily injury liability, Property damage liability, Personal injury protection (PIP), Comprehensive coverage, Collision coverage, Uninsured/underinsured motorist, Rental reimbursement and roadside assistance
  - Key Benefits: Compliance with Florida insurance requirements, Financial protection after accidents or vehicle damage, Coverage options that fit your driving habits and vehicle type, Peace of mind whether driving locally or long-distance

  **Home**
  - Description: Protecting your home means protecting more than just the structure. It's about safeguarding what matters most.
  - Coverage Options: Dwelling (home structure), Other structures (garages, sheds, fences), Personal property, Loss of use and additional living expenses, Personal liability, Medical payments to others, Optional endorsements for water damage/equipment breakdown/increased limits for valuables
  - Key Benefits: Financial protection against covered losses, Liability protection for accidents on your property, Coverage tailored to your home/location/lifestyle, Optional enhancements to address Florida-specific risks

  **Health**
  - Description: Health insurance helps cover medical expenses and supports access to necessary care.
  - Coverage Options: Individual and family health plans, ACA-compliant plans, Supplemental health coverage, Dental and vision options, Short-term medical plans (where available)
  - Key Benefits: Help managing healthcare costs, Access to a network of medical providers, Coverage options tailored to individual or family needs, Guidance through plan selection and enrollment

  **Life**
  - Description: Life insurance helps provide financial security for those who depend on you.
  - Coverage Options: Term life insurance, Whole life insurance, Universal life insurance, Final expense coverage, Coverage for income replacement/debt payoff/legacy planning
  - Key Benefits: Financial support for loved ones, Flexible options based on goals and budget, Ability to plan for both short- and long-term needs, Peace of mind knowing your family is protected

  **Flood**
  - Description: Flood insurance provides vital protection that is not typically included in standard homeowner policies.
  - Coverage Options: Building coverage, Contents coverage, Policies through the National Flood Insurance Program (NFIP), Private flood insurance options
  - Key Benefits: Financial protection from flood-related damage, Coverage availability inside and outside high-risk flood zones, Options for broader limits depending on the policy, Added peace of mind for Florida property owners
